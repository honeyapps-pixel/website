Fantasy Manager · Effective date: July 16, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal data when you use the Fantasy Manager mobile application and website (together, the "Service"). Fantasy Manager is an AI-assisted analytics tool for fantasy football, provided for informational and entertainment purposes only. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the NFL, Sleeper, or any team, league, or player.

3. Data we collect

Account & profile

  • Email address and authentication identifier (via email, Sign in with Apple, or Google Sign-In). With Sign in with Apple you may share only Apple's private relay address.
  • Display name and, if provided, profile picture.
  • Your Sleeper username and user ID when you connect a Sleeper account.
  • Subscription tier and in-app preferences and settings.

Fantasy football data

  • Leagues, rosters, matchups, transactions, and related data retrieved from the Sleeper API for the accounts you connect.
  • Your interactions with AI features (questions, requested analyses, lineup and trade inputs).

Usage, device & technical data

  • Product usage and event data to operate and improve the Service.
  • Device information (model, operating system, app version) and, if you opt in, a push-notification token.
  • Server, security, and diagnostic logs, including IP address, processed by our infrastructure providers to deliver and secure the Service.

Subscription & purchase data

  • Purchase and subscription status. In-app purchases are processed by Apple and managed via RevenueCat. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

We do not knowingly collect special categories of data (Art. 9 GDPR); please do not submit such information through the Service.

4. Purposes and legal bases

Provision of the Service (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR – performance of a contract):

  • Creating your account, connecting leagues, and generating rankings, lineups, trade and waiver analysis.
  • Processing your fantasy inputs to generate AI-assisted output.
  • Verifying purchases and unlocking paid features.

Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR):

  • Push notifications, if you enable them. You can withdraw consent in your device settings at any time.

Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR):

  • Improving the Service, analytics, abuse prevention, rate limiting, and support.
  • Security and fraud prevention.

Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR): compliance with legal obligations and enforcement of our terms.

5. AI processing

Some features send the relevant fantasy context (for example your roster, matchup, or question) to our AI processor to generate a response. We do not send your email, password, or payment details to the AI provider. AI-generated output can be inaccurate or incomplete and is provided for entertainment only — see our Terms of Service.

7. Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active and as needed to provide the Service. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymise your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we must retain certain records to comply with legal obligations (e.g. tax or accounting) or to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims. Backups are purged on a rolling schedule.

8. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access (Art. 15) – information about the data we hold.
  • Rectification (Art. 16) – correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure (Art. 17) – you can delete your account in-app or by contacting us.
  • Restriction (Art. 18) of processing.
  • Data portability (Art. 20) – you can export your data in the app under Settings.
  • Objection (Art. 21) to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdrawal of consent (Art. 7(3)) at any time.

To exercise your rights, contact info@honeyapps.de. We will respond without undue delay and within 30 days (Art. 12(3) GDPR).

9. Security & data storage

We use technical and organisational measures appropriate to the risk, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), row-level access controls in our database, secure on-device token storage (Keychain / encrypted storage), and least-privilege access. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. Your primary data is hosted in the EU.

10. Local storage & cookies

The mobile app uses secure device storage to keep you signed in and cache data for offline use. The website uses strictly necessary cookies and local storage for authentication and core functionality; these are required and are not used for cross-site advertising.

11. Children

Fantasy Manager is directed at persons who have reached the minimum age for valid consent to data processing in their country (in Germany: 16; per Art. 8 GDPR). We do not knowingly collect personal data from persons below that age. If we learn that we have, we will delete it without undue delay.

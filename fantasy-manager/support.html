Frequently asked questions

How do I connect my Sleeper account?
Open the app, go to Settings, and enter your Sleeper username to import your leagues and rosters.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are billed by Apple. Open the App Store app → your account → Subscriptions → Fantasy Manager to change or cancel. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

How do I restore my purchase?
On the paywall screen, tap "Restore Purchases" while signed in to the same Apple ID used for the purchase.

How do I delete my account and data?
In the app under Settings, or email us at info@honeyapps.de. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Are the AI rankings guaranteed?
No. Rankings and recommendations are AI/statistical estimates for entertainment only and are not a guarantee of any result. See our Terms of Service.
